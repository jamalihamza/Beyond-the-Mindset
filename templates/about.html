About Beyond the Mindset

Project Overview

"Beyond the Mindset" is a web platform designed to empower individuals in managing their physical well-being through specialized health calculators. Currently, users can access tools for assessing health metrics, such as BMI, BMR, body fat percentage, sleep cycle, and more, with each calculator featuring a dedicated design and explanations for comprehensive insights into individual health.

Inspiration

"Beyond the Mindset" was inspired by a desire to create a reliable, user-centered platform for personal health management. With influences from reputable sources, including NHS, NIH, Huberman Lab, and Built with Science, the project aims to provide an approachable yet powerful resource for understanding and improving health.

Features

  • Health Calculators: Calculate essential health metrics such as BMI, BMR, body fat percentage, sleep cycle, and more. Each calculator includes a description, calculation interface, and relevant health articles to deepen user understanding.
  • Unique Design for Each Calculator: Each health calculator is designed with its own CSS and JavaScript, ensuring a streamlined and user-friendly experience.

Technologies Used

  • Frontend Design: Developed with Webflow to ensure a professional and dynamic user experience.
  • Backend: Developed using Flask on CS50.dev.
  • JavaScript & CSS: Custom styling and functionality for each calculator.
  • Resources & Inspiration:

    • Refined Guides, as an inspiration for BMI and BMR calculators
    • GymNation, Calculator.net for further health calculator ideas.

Future Improvements

Planned expansions for "Beyond the Mindset" include interactive mental health assessments, a blog repository focused on health and wellness topics, and comprehensive user profiles for progress tracking. These enhancements aim to provide a full spectrum of wellness tools, ultimately transforming the platform into an all-encompassing resource for mental and physical health.
